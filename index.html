Beaches

The city of Cádiz as a whole is also a very popular tourist attraction due to its numerous beaches between the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea and its warm temperatures. The most popular ones are La Caleta Beach, La Victoria Beach, and Santa María del Mar Beach. In all of them, you will find many restaurants serving traditional seafood. The beaches of Cádiz are also considered the best as they have very fine, white sand.

Traditions

The carnival of Cadiz is one of the most famous around the world. Together with the Tenerife and Murcia carnivals, it was declared a Festival of International Tourist Interest. It takes place during the months of January and February. It is said that the beginnings of the carnival are of Christian origin: before Lent (40 days before Easter). That is why they created this colorful and joyful celebration. During all these days, you can enjoy the "pregones", "tablaos", masked dances, parades, "exornos", "concurso de agrupaciones" and fireworks. There are five prizes for each modality and are different categories (adults, youth and children).

Best Hotels

Boutique Hotel Casa Cánovas is an upscale hotel located in the old town of Cádiz, Spain. The hotel offers ornate rooms and suites, some of which feature balconies, providing a warm and luxurious atmosphere. The hotel is situated in a great location, within walking distance to most tourist attractions, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking to explore the area.
